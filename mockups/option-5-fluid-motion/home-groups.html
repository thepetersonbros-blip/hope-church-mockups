Home Groups

Growing relationships around the Word, all across Savannah.

Why Home Groups?

Life Together, Around the Word

Hope home groups exist to glorify God through the intentional and disciplined gathering of people for the purpose of living out the biblical teachings of scripture in the context of growing relationships. In our home groups we fellowship together by spending time processing the sermon and discussing ways to apply it to our lives. Ultimately, we want to be better equipped to apply the Word of God to our lives so that we go out and share the good news of Jesus Christ with the world. If this interests you, please reach out to one of our home group leaders for details regarding logistics, as each group is different.
