Care and Counseling

God is deeply present.

At Hope we understand life sometimes involves pain and suffering. We believe God is deeply present with us in our sufferings, is the provider of all comfort, and enables us to comfort those around us who are suffering. We consider it a high honor to walk with people through their pain and suffering as we engage the Scriptures together to seek comfort from God and transformation through His Spirit.

At No Cost

We provide care and counsel at no cost in all areas including, but not limited to:

  • anxiety
  • fear
  • conflict
  • grief & loss
  • addictive behaviors
  • anger management
  • abuse
  • intimacy
  • divorce
  • stress
  • infidelity
  • marriage
  • depression
  • parenting
  • guilt
  • self-harm
