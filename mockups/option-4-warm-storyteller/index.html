Welcome to Hope Bible Church

Are you new to Hope Bible Church?

Hope Bible Church gathers weekly to actively participate in worship together. The goal of our gathering is to direct hearts and minds toward our King Jesus Christ and to equip the saints to go out and do the work of ministry on His behalf. We do this through weekly preaching, singing, prayer and participation in the Lord’s Supper. We’d love to have you join us on Sunday!

Care and Counseling

Hope for real life

Sometimes life hurts. At times we all need help understanding and dealing with suffering in our lives as a result of living in a broken world. We believe the Bible has hope for you. The counselors at Hope Bible Church would love to help you. Interested in getting care?
